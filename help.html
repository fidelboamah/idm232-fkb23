How to search for a recipe


Click on the menu button in the top-left corner of the page
Select the "On the menu" tab to visit the search page
On that page you can search accroding to the available filters "sweet, spicy, hot or vegan"
You can also search using words, just type in the search box and hit the search icon when you're done. The web app will process your request and reload the screen with what you're looking for.
